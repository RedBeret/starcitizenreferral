üåü Your First Steps in Star Citizen

After Installing the Game:

  1. Choose Your Starting Location
    Area 18 (ArcCorp) - Industrial city, good for missions
    New Babbage (microTech) - Beautiful snowy planet
    Lorville (Hurston) - Large city with varied missions
    Orison (Crusader) - Cloud city, stunning views
  2. Wake Up in Your Hab
    Take time to learn movement controls
    Press F to interact with objects
    Hold F for interaction wheel
  3. Find the Spaceport
    Follow signs or ask in chat (F12)
    Take the train/shuttle to the spaceport
  4. Spawn Your Ship
    Use ASOP terminals at the spaceport
    Request your ship and note the hangar/pad number
  5. Take Your First Flight
    Enter ship (hold F, select "Open" then "Enter")
    Press R to flight ready
    Hold Space to take off

Essential Keybinds to Know:

  • F - Interact / Inner Thought
  • R - Flight Ready
  • Space - Strafe Up / Takeoff
  • Ctrl - Strafe Down / Land
  • B - Quantum Travel
  • F1 - MobiGlas (Map/Missions)
  • F12 - Chat Window
  • Tab - Scan Mode
