‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

When do I receive my 50,000 UEC?

The 50,000 UEC bonus is reserved for the official game launch. It's tied to your account immediately upon using a referral code during signup, but will only become available when Star Citizen releases. During alpha testing, you start with 20,000 aUEC instead.

What's the difference between UEC and aUEC?

UEC (final game): Your 50,000 bonus, available at launch, never wiped, can be purchased.
aUEC (alpha testing): Start with 20,000, usable NOW, may be wiped in updates, cannot be purchased with real money.

Can I get 50,000 UEC on an existing account?

Only if your account is less than 24 hours old. After 24 hours, referral codes cannot be added retroactively.

Do I lose aUEC if my character dies?

No, aUEC stays in your account. You only lose items you're physically carrying if you don't recover your body within the timer.

What happens to aUEC during database wipes?

Earned aUEC may be reset during major patches. However, your 50,000 UEC referral bonus is permanently tied to your account and will be available at game launch regardless of wipes.

Can I use my 50,000 UEC now in alpha?

No. The 50,000 UEC is for the final game release. During alpha, you work with aUEC (starting with 20,000 aUEC). Think of your UEC bonus as a "launch day gift" waiting for you!
